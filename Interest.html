Explore and Enjoy with Ease

Mianus River Park

389-acre forest with rugged mountain biking & hiking trails, fishing spots & lots of dog walkers.

Devils Den Preserve

This 1,756-acre property features a variety of habitats & trails for wildlife viewing & hiking.

Saugatuck Universal Access Trail

The trail is about 500 feet in length and ends at a platform overlooking the Saugatuck Reservoir. It is open from sunrise to sunset.

Norwalk River Valley Trail (NRVT)

The Norwalk River Valley Trail is a multi-use trail. The plan for the trail is to run from Norwalk, Connecticut to Danbury, Connecticut stretching about 38 miles.

Topstone Park

Forested 280-acre park with a picturesque lake used for swimming & kayaking, plus nature trails.

Larkin State Bridle Trail

Picturesque 10.3-mile trail along a former railroad used for horse riding, cycling & hiking.

Bennetts Pond State Park

The park features the 56-acre pond for which it is named and many miles of hiking trails in a pristine woodland environment.

West Rock Ridge State Park

A 627-ft. ridge offers views of New Haven Harbor & Long Island Sound, with hiking, fishing & more.

Pequonnock River Valley State Park

State park with a river valley carved by melting glacial waters, leaving notable granite formations.
